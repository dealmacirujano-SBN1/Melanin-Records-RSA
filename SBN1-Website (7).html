The Sonic Surgeon

Tumelo M. Makwarela

Medical Doctor, HPCSA professional, and architect of a sound he calls "Deep-Soulful-Amapiano." Hailing from Soweto (Dobsonville/Braamfischer) and Vosloorus.

Over a decade into his journey, his discography bridges South African House heritage with the future of electronic music. The 2026 album Khathutshelo and the acclaimed Soul of Kwaito series are testaments to his mastery.

Every track is a tribute to his late brother, Khathu — and a gift to those who find healing in the rhythm.
